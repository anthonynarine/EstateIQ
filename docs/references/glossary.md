# Glossary

## Organization
A landlord business within the SaaS platform.

## Building
A physical property within an organization’s portfolio.

## Unit
A rentable space inside a building.

## Lease
The contract that attaches a tenant to a unit for a period.

## Charge
A receivable entry representing money owed.

## Payment
A cash receipt entry representing money received.

## Allocation
A link that applies part of a payment to a charge.

## Expense
An operating cost recorded against the organization, building, unit, or lease.

## Delinquency
The state of unpaid charges as of a given date, usually bucketed by age.

## Org scoping
The rule that every data access must stay within the active organization boundary.
