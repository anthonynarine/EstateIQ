# ICP and Jobs-to-be-Done

## Ideal customer profile

- Owns or manages **1–50 residential units**
- Often family-run or self-managed
- Uses a messy mix of bank apps, spreadsheets, receipts, and text messages
- Needs financial clarity more than enterprise property-management bloat

## Core jobs-to-be-done

1. **Know what I make**
   - net cash flow
   - building-level profitability
   - portfolio performance

2. **Know what is owed**
   - delinquency
   - aging
   - tenant payment history

3. **Know what I spent**
   - expense categories
   - building and unit cost burden
   - receipts and supporting documentation

4. **Prepare for taxes**
   - clean exports
   - accountant-ready records
   - year-end packaging

5. **Make better decisions**
   - later: scenarios, simulations, and AI summaries
