# Pricing Hypothesis

## Initial working hypothesis

- Up to 10 units: **$29/mo**
- 11–25 units: **$49/mo**
- 26–50 units: **$79/mo**

## Positioning logic

The pricing is built around a small-landlord market that is:
- underserved by institutional software
- too complex for spreadsheets
- highly sensitive to trust and ease of use

## Product promise

Give small landlords the kind of financial visibility larger operators already have,
without forcing them into enterprise-grade complexity.
